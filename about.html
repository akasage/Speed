Welcome to CUALA

Covenant University Alumni Association can trace her history as far back as Saturday, July 29, 2006. At an investiture program held in the University Chapel, the alumni association was inaugurated and commissioned by the Chancellor, Dr. David Oyedepo in the presence of the over 720 graduates who formed the first set of ‘day-old eagles’.

The first set of leaders was appointed by the Chancellor as caretakers of the association pending the appointment of elected leaders of the association. Members of the caretaker committee were Mr. Muyiwa Fadugba, Mr. Reginald Bassey, Miss Morenike Oso, Miss Ada Sonia Obanua, and Mr. Bayo Akomolafe.
